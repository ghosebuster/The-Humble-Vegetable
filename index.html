About us

The Humble Vegetable is run by The Cabbage Patch Kids - a group of London-based vegetable enthusiasts. We love veg and we want to share that love through this website. Whether you are already a veg expert or are just starting out your journey into the wonderful world of veg, we hope that you can learn a little from us and be inspired to Grow, Cook and Eat more vegetables.
